Nikola Tesla

Inventor - Engineer - Physics

Tesla was a Serbian-American inventor, electrical engineer, mechanical engineer, and futurist who is best known for his contributions to the design of the modern alternating current (AC) electricity supply system.

Some of his inventions include:

Tesla Coil

It is used to produce high-voltage, low-current, high frequency alternating-current electricity.Today, their main usage is for entertainment and educational displays

Radio

Radio is very widely used in modern technology, in radio communication, radar, radio navigation, remote control, remote sensing and other applications

Turbina Tesla

Bioengineering researchers have referred to it as a multiple-disk centrifugal pump. One of Tesla's desires for implementation of this turbine was for geothermal power, which was described in Our Future Motive Power
